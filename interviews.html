Before the Interview

  • Research the company’s mission, culture, and products.
  • Review the job description carefully and match your skills to it.
  • Prepare a clear introduction about yourself (your "elevator pitch").

During the Interview

  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to answer questions.
  • Showcase teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills.
  • Ask thoughtful questions about the role and company.

After the Interview

  • Send a thank-you email within 24 hours.
  • Reflect on your performance and note areas of improvement.
  • Follow up politely if you don’t hear back in 1–2 weeks.
