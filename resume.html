Core Principles

  • One page for most students; clear headings and consistent formatting.
  • Action-oriented bullet points using strong verbs and measurable outcomes.
  • Prioritize the most relevant experiences for your target roles.
  • Include key skills, tools, and certifications recruiters search for.

Suggested Structure

  1. Header: Name, email, phone, LinkedIn/GitHub (as applicable).
  2. Education: Degree, school, graduation date, relevant coursework.
  3. Experience: Role, organization, location, dates, 3–5 impact bullets.
  4. Projects: Links and results; show your contributions and outcomes.
  5. Skills & Certifications: Group by category (e.g., Languages, Tools).
